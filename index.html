About

Tsum Tsum is a mobile puzzle solving game. You can send hearts to friends to play more games and earn coins. By earning coins, you can purchase more Tsum Tsum. When you purchase the same Tsum Tsum a certain amount of times, the Level of that Tsum Tsum becomes stronger, and you can earn more points and create higher combos. By doing so, you can earn high scores and move up the leaderboards when you can challenge your friends.
